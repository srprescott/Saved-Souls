Adopt

Please fill out and submit our Adoption Application. Once the application is submitted, it will be reviewed and, if approved, you will be contacted for a meet and greet. All of our dogs are adopted into BEST FIT homes - not on a first come, first serve basis. If there are multiple applicants for one dog, the best fit for that dog will be chosen and everybody will be informed of the decision.

Adoption fees include, but are not limited to, age-appropriate vaccinations, dewormings, a visit with a licensed veterinarian for a wellness checkup, microchipping and spay/neuter. Adoption fees may vary dependent on situation but our standard adoption fee is $250.

Puppies under 6 months of age will be adopted out with an alteration contract to be upheld at 6 months of age. Dogs 7 months and older are subject to a one week trial period - this is explained further in the adoption application.

Foster

Fill out and submit our foster application. It will be reviewed and determined if you would be accepted as a foster for our rescue. If accepted, your information (name and address) will be shared with the Department of Agriculture as a foster for our rescue, as required by the state. From there, you will be an active foster for our rescue! The foster application does let you specify breeds and ages of dogs that you would be most comfortable with fostering!

You will always be asked ahead of time if you will be available to foster a dog (unless you ask/commit to a specific dog) - but you will never, EVER be pressured to take a dog (even if you commit to fostering it - I understand things change)! Fosters always have first option to adopt so long as the dog would be an appropriate long-term fit for the home.

The rescue provides anything and everything needed to fosters including, but not limited to, crates, food, toys, leashes and collars, bath items, all vetting, etc - there is a section on the foster app to mark what you will need for your foster dog(s)! Fostering dogs is extremely rewarding and really helps save lives!

Transport

Please send us a message to let us know you are interested in transporting for our rescue - include your email address. A transport application will be emailed to you. Once filled out and submitted, it will be reviewed and, if approved, you will be contacted and informed and it will be filed in our “Transporters” file. If a dog is ever in need of transport, you (and other approved transporters) will be contacted to see if anybody is available to assist in transport.

Donate

We are a non-profit, 501c3 rescue. Any donations are tax-deductible! That said, I will provide a receipt for any donations as well as keep a copy for the rescue records. Monetary contributions are absolutely welcome as well. Contributions to their care/supplies can be sent via PayPal to savedsouls@yahoo.com. Contributions can also be called directly into our vet: Big Creek Vet Hospital at (440) 639-8585 under Saved Souls Dog Rescue, Inc. or to the facility where our dogs are fixed: Petfix 216-732-7040 under Saved Souls Dog Rescue, Inc.

Contribute

Items that are always needed are: leashes, collars, dog bowls, baby pools/raised dog beds, cleaning supplies (paper towels, spray bottles, simple green cleaner, all-natural cleaners (Mrs. Meyers) to avoid chemical burns on pads), any crates of all sizes (medium and large more so), puppy pads, newspapers (not the shiny inserts), dog and puppy food (preferably grain free), treats, linens of all kinds (not washed in Tide as I am allergic), laundry detergent and softener (again, no Tide) as well as unscented dryer sheets, bath supplies (towels, baby shampoo, grooming wipes/sprays), dog treats, toys, Kongs, soup bones (uncooked - sold at butcher shops/Giant Eagle), peanut butter, hygiene supplies (doggy nail clippers, baby wipes for ears, epsom salts for soaking any foot wounds, gauze, ace wraps, self adhering wraps) and any other doggy-related items. If you aren’t sure if we could use it, just ask!
